### When I bought this S4, the previous owner had replaced the stock exhaust with an aftermarket crossover pipe and straight pipes from there back. No mufflers, no resonators. I'm sure it sounded exactly as antisocial as you'd imagine. I sourced a second-hand OEM system and set out to put it back to the way Audi intended.

#### I know, buying a performance car and making it quieter. I stand by it.

![2012 Audi S4 jacked up from the rear, work underway](/assets/img/Mechanical Experience/2012 Audi S4/2024-11-12/20241112_131703.jpg){: width="100%" }

<br>

#### Getting under the car to pull the crossover pipe, I found it had corroded and burned through completely. Not that it was doing much before anyway. You can also spot the Bilstein yellows here, which I had installed in a separate session, front and rear struts in a single eight-hour day.

![Underside view showing the crossover pipe and suspension](/assets/img/Mechanical Experience/2012 Audi S4/2024-11-12/20241112_131715.jpg){: width="100%" }

<br>

#### The full OEM cat-back laid out on the driveway. Y-pipe, resonator sections, and the dual mufflers with quad tips. This is what the car was missing. It's a genuinely nice exhaust for a factory system.

![Full OEM exhaust system laid out on the driveway](/assets/img/Mechanical Experience/2012 Audi S4/2024-11-12/20241112_142604.jpg){: width="100%" }

<br>

#### The section upstream of the break was otherwise in good shape. Rather than source another tube, I cut it back past the failure point, offset the cut from the mating section to leave room for a sleeve and clamps, and coupled the two together. About $100 in wrecker parts and $50 in hardware, which is cheap by this car's standards.

![Repaired mid-pipe section cut and prepped for coupling](/assets/img/Mechanical Experience/2012 Audi S4/2024-11-12/20241112_150741.jpg){: width="100%" }

<br>

### The rain moved in before I could get a final shot, but the system went on clean. The car is noticeably more pleasant to live with, and pulling up somewhere in a stock-looking S4 that nobody hears coming suits me just fine.
