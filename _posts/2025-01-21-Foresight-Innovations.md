### The Project

Foresight Innovations is my dad's retirement passion project, an IT company he runs out of the house. He periodically gets me involved for everything from general computer troubleshooting to purpose-built hardware. When he picked up a Bambu A1, I had it unboxed, assembled, and printing its first part inside 30 minutes. From there the requests have kept coming.

So far that has meant designing and printing monitor arm logo caps with the Foresight Innovations branding, a set of mini PC mounts for his server rack, and various other printed hardware to keep the setup clean and organized. The server rack itself is black extruded aluminum with most of the structural and mounting components printed, a few of which I designed myself. All of it was modelled in Fusion 360 and printed on his A1.
