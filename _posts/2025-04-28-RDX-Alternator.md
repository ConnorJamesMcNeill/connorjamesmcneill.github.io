### The 2015 RDX runs a 3.5L J35Y4 SOHC V6, a naturally aspirated Honda J-series making 279 hp and 252 lb-ft. It's a smooth, reliable unit in normal operation. My cousin's was not in normal operation when she called from the side of the highway coming back from Banff. The charging system had packed it in, the battery drained down, and the car died under her. My uncle and I drove out to recover her, and the following weekend we had it back to his garage to sort out.

### A quick check confirmed the alternator was the culprit. Honda quoted a number that nobody wanted to pay, and that was that. I still have staff pricing at the parts store near my house from when I worked there, which makes keeping costs down a lot easier. I sourced an aftermarket reman unit and got to work. The J35Y4's engine bay is reasonably well packaged for a transversely-mounted V6 and the alternator isn't buried, but there's still a fair amount to move out of the way to get clean access.
